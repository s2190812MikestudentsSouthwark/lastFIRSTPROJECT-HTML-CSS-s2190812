Our services

SERVICES OFFERED BY THE PLATFORM ID verification to match a wallet address to a real person
Connecting people accessing the platform
On-chain escrow service with multisign account
Badge generation to measure reputation Dispute settlement for the payments occurred via the escrow service
Freelancers are identified by a wallet address which is linked to their ID as verified by the Platform

Clients can access the platform to search for the freelance services they need
Once the Client and the freelancer have reached their agreement, the Client shall pay the agreed amount with crypto in an on-chain escrow account (multisign) managed by the Platform
Once the Client receives the work, the Client shall unlock the amount in the escrow
If the escrow is unlocked by the Client, a reward token generated by the Platform is sent to the freelancer
Once the freelancer gets a certain amount of reward tokens, a badge is minted to state the reputation
If the Client does not unlock the escrow, the platform can unlock the escrow without assigning a reward token to the freelancer and retaining a fee for the dispute settling
